About Electro-Fluidics

Electro-Fluidics is a specialized consultancy and R&D platform dedicated to developing integrated fluidic flow cells for sustainable electrochemical technologies. We specialize in investigating electrode–electrolyte and membrane–electrolyte interfaces to overcome mass transport limitations in energy storage and sensing systems.

Our Services

Stack Engineering

Analyzing mass transport, shunt currents, and system losses in flow batteries and electrolyzers to identify bottlenecks and pathways for cost reduction.

Interfacial Modeling

Expertise in **electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (EIS)** and equivalent circuit modeling to understand relaxation processes at electrified interfaces.

CAD & Prototyping

Leveraging **Autodesk Fusion 360** for the design and 3D printing of customized flow cells, Luer-lock interfaces, and profiled membranes.

Applications

  • **Energy Storage:** Acid-base flow batteries and lithium-ion systems.
  • **Bioprocess Monitoring:** Real-time sensing of pH, dissolved oxygen, and biomarkers in miniaturized fermentors.
  • **Sustainability:** Electrification of industrial operations through CO₂ conversion and biomass dewatering.

Products & Prototypes

Our current portfolio includes **3D-printed profiled membranes** designed to enhance micro-level mixing and **miniaturized electrochemical sensor arrays** for diagnostic applications.
