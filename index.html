About Us

Electro-Fluidics is a specialized consultancy and R&D platform dedicated to developing integrated fluidic flow cells for sustainable electrochemical technologies. We investigate electrode–electrolyte interfaces to overcome mass transport limitations and scale laboratory breakthroughs into industrial solutions.
