About Electro-Fluidics

Electro-Fluidics is a specialized consultancy and R&D platform dedicated to developing integrated fluidic flow cells for sustainable electrochemical technologies. We specialize in investigating electrode–electrolyte and membrane–electrolyte interfaces to overcome mass transport limitations in energy storage and sensing systems.

Our Services

Stack Engineering

Analyzing mass transport, shunt currents, and system losses in batteries, electrolyzers, and electrodialysis to identify bottlenecks and pathways for cost reduction.

Interfacial Analysis & EIS

Expertise in Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS) and equivalent circuit modelling to analyze the aging behavior of batteries and the electrical characterization of biomolecules. We solve complex interface challenges through advanced kinetic modeling.

CAD & Prototyping

Leveraging CAD design and 3D printing of customized flow cells, fluidic interfaces, and profiled membranes.

Applications

Energy Storage

Optimizing aqueous Flow Batteries and micro capacitors arrays by improving membrane-electrolyte interface stability and ion transport efficiency.

Bioprocess Monitoring

Developing Integrated Biosensors for real-time detection of metabolites in complex fluidic environments, focusing on sensitivity and fouling resistance.

Industrial Electrolysis

Solving mass transport limitations in CO2 Electrolyzers and Electrodialysis stacks to enable high-current density operation at scale.
